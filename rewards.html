Core Rule

Characters gain power through reward cards instead of normal class levels. A Level-Up Reward offers 3 random reward cards and the player chooses 1.

Reward category decides what kind of reward it is. Rarity decides how strong it is for the current floor.

Boss Pool Logic

Boss rewards use the reward pool of the floor the party is entering.

  • Floor 1 Boss uses Floor 2 rewards.
  • Floor 2 Boss uses Floor 3 rewards.
  • Floor 3 Boss uses Final rewards.

Rarity Tables

Dungeon: 55% Common, 30% Uncommon, 11% Rare, 3% Very Rare, 1% Legendary

Elite: 30% Common, 42% Uncommon, 18% Rare, 7% Very Rare, 3% Legendary

Boss: 40% Uncommon, 40% Rare, 14% Very Rare, 6% Legendary

Final Boss: 50% Rare, 32% Very Rare, 18% Legendary
